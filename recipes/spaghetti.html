Spaghetti

Description

A very basic and budget friendly spagbol I like to cook.

A good dish for fussy little ones as veggies can be easily hidden in it.

It's easy and quick to put together and most ingredients can be adjusted.

Ingredients

    Base:

  • Spalsh of olive oil
  • 1 red/brown onion - diced
  • 1 clove of garlic - minced
  • 500g beef mince
  • 400g tin crushed tomatoes
  • 700g (bottle) passata
  • 500g spaghetti pasta
  • Dried oregano
  • Fresh/dried basil
  • Grated cheese

    • Optional:

  • Carrots - diced/grated
  • Zuchini - diced/grated
  • Mushrooms - diced

How to prepare

  1. Cook onions and carrots (if diced) for a couple minutes over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
  3. Add mince to the pan and break it down with spatula.
  4. Season with salt and cook it until browned. Stir frequently.
  5. (Add optional ingredients, if used.)
  6. Add the crushed tomatoes, pour in the passata and add the herbs/spices.
  7. Stir everything together and let it come to a simmer.
  8. Let it simmer for at least 15 minutes. Stir as you go and adjust taste.
  9. Cook pasta while this simmers
  10. Garnish with your favourite cheese.
